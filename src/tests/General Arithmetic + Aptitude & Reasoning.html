Question No: 9

Direction: If “+” is “×”, “–” is “+”, “×” is “÷” and “÷” is “–”, then answer the following question based on this information: 21 ÷ 8 + 2 – 12 × 3 =?

Options:

  • 1. 11
  • 2. 9
  • 3. 19
  • 4. 14

Solution:

  • To solve this problem, first we need to set the signs that has been given in the equation:

    + is ×, – is +, × is ÷ and ÷ is –

    So after rearranging the signs, the equation will be:

    21 – 8 × 2 + 12 ÷ 3

    So, solving this, 21 – 8 × 2 + 12 ÷ 3

    21 – 8 × 2 + 4

    21 – 16 + 4

    25 – 16 = 9

    Hence, the correct answer is 9.

Question No: 14

Ayushman was born two years after his father’s marriage. His mother is five years younger than his father, but 20 years older than Ayushman who is 10 years old. At what age did the father get married?

Options:

  • 1. 23 years
  • 2. 25 years
  • 3. 33 years
  • 4. 35 years

Solution:

  • Ayushman’s present age = 10 years, His mother’s present age = (10 + 20) years = 30 years.

    Ayushman’s father’s present age = (30 + 5) years = 35 years.

    Ayushman’s father’s age at the time of Ayushman’s birth = (35 – 10) years = 25 years

    ∴ Ayushman’s father’s age at the time of marriage = (25 – 2) years = 23 years
