This is undertaken as part of the reproductive maternal neonatal child and adolescent health (RMNCH+A) strategy.     • Option D: A cash incentive of 1400 rupees is provided to all pregnant women who underwent an institutional delivery in low-performing states under Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY). This scheme is launched under NRHM (national rural health mission) to promote institutional delivery.
